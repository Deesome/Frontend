PROGRAMS

Gurukul Program
Our Gurukul Program is a unique and intensive learning experience designed for students dedicated to academic excellence. From 8 AM to 8 PM, students immerse themselves in a structured environment that combines classroom learning, self-study, and real-time doubt resolution. This program ensures that students stay on track, with no chance of backlogs. With a balanced routine of guided instruction and personal study time, students can strengthen their knowledge, address doubts immediately, and stay ahead in their preparation.

Mentor-Mentee Program
Our Mentor-Mentee Program is a personalized initiative designed to guide students toward academic success. Each student is assigned a mentor who tracks their progress, addresses challenges, and keeps them focused on their goals. The program fosters a structured approach where mentors help students manage study schedules, resolve doubts, and overcome obstacles. Regular check-ins and performance tracking ensure tailored guidance, enabling students to improve continuously. With real-time feedback and personalized strategies, students can build confidence, enhance their understanding, and stay committed to their preparation. This program ensures every student receives the support needed to achieve their academic aspirations
