PROGRAMS

Gurukul Program
Our Gurukul Program is a unique and intensive learning experience designed for students dedicated to academic excellence. From 8 AM to 8 PM, students immerse themselves in a structured environment that combines classroom learning, self-study, and real-time doubt resolution. This program ensures that students stay on track, with no chance of backlogs. With a balanced routine of guided instruction and personal study time, students can strengthen their knowledge, address doubts immediately, and stay ahead in their preparation.

Mentor-Mentee Program
Our Mentor-Mentee Program is designed to provide personalized guidance and support to each student. Every student is assigned a dedicated mentor who monitors their progress, addresses their challenges, and ensures they stay on the right academic path. Mentors play an active role in helping students manage their study schedules, resolve doubts, and tackle any obstacles that arise in their learning journey. By maintaining regular check-ins, tracking performance, and offering tailored advice, mentors ensure that no student is left behind. This program offers students the opportunity to strengthen their understanding, receive real-time feedback, and build confidence in their preparation. With the mentor's continuous support, students can optimize their learning experience, overcome academic hurdles, and stay focused on their goals.

ESOS Program
ESOS is a powerful tool for tracking each student’s academic progress, attendance, and participation. It helps mentors and teachers identify strengths and areas for improvement, providing personalized feedback and support. By regularly monitoring performance, ESOS ensures timely interventions, allowing every student to stay on track and succeed in their studies. Join the Program and benefit from personalized progress tracking!
